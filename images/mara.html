        Wildlife Balloon (Safari) Flight

        The Balloon (Safari) / Flight and Breakfast in the bush was wonderful. It was a once in a lifetime experience.

        The flight takes off at day break and you have a breathtaking bird's eye view of the African wildlife having their breakfast or hunting for their meal. We viewed lions eating their catch of the morning and also a pack of Hyenas savoring their recent meat conquest.

        At the conclusion of the unforgettable flight the pilot landed (putting it mildly) the balloon in a field, yes a field where we had the best meal of the trip. Some of the local Maasai Tribesmen, now working for the resort prepared the best breakfast you can imagine accompanied by sparkling wine. The ambiance of the meal was absolutely wonderful. The landing of the craft is something you must experience for yourself.

        The skillful piloting of the air craft was performed by Donates Stakeliunas. The experience and expert handling of the balloon by Donates makes you feel safe every minute, from preflight instructions to the touchdown of the craft.

        I would highly recommend everyone to enjoy the experience of a Balloon Flight while on holiday in Africa. You will never forget the experience. You may only have the chance once in a lifetime so take advantage of the opportunity.

        Enjoy Life.

        Lloyd Adams
        Baytown, Texas
